Description

Shackle is a VR horror game in which two players must communicate with one another to solve disjoint puzzles.

In the game, you and your friend have been captured and bound back-to-back in chairs, and you must work together to escape your bonds. However, you also must be careful not to disturb the monster hiding in the environment; it will very quickly go from trickster to nightmare.
This is the most extensive project that I have worked on, complete with copious amounts of playtesting, agile development practices and social media presence.
