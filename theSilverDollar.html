Description

In this realistic adventure game, you experience the mundane in extraordinary ways by playing as a preteen with an overactive imagination.

This is a first person point of view (POV) adventure game. Using nothing more than running, jumping, crawling and items you find out in the field, you conquer challenges and proceed on your quest to find the main character's (Zack Sander's) lost silver dollar. Being observant, taking in your surroundings, and moving deliberately is often more important than making quick decisions, however, fast-paced sequences exist as well.

All aspects of gameplay are as Zach would see the world. Because of this, events during gameplay always have a fantastic spin on them, meaning virtually anything, from superheroes and rainbows to darkness and monsters, can show up in the game. However, all things in gameplay correspond to something real outside of Zachâ€™s mind. This is shown through subtle similarities in appearance and sound between imaginary and real-world objects. For the most part, people will retain the same voice in the imaginary world that they have in the real one. Similarly, distinctive behaviors and/or clothing items will also show up on the character in both worlds.
