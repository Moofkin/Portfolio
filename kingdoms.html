Description

Kingdoms is a tabletop military strategy/resource gathering game that was designed and prototyped by myself and three other people. Our intention was to create a game that could be left out on a table and played without all the participants in the game present.

Kingdoms can be played with anywhere from 6 to 24 players, and supports drop-in players as well (assuming there are still less than 24 people in the game). Players are grouped into 6 teams of 4, with multiple people filling multiple spots on each team if necessary. Each team consists of a king, a general, and two gatherers. These players must strategize together to successfully run their kingdom. It is also encouraged for different kingdoms to secretly form alliances and/or work together to knock other kingdoms out of the game. The last one standing wins.
