Description

Potential for Life is a strategy/resource gathering game that was designed, prototyped, and created from scratch by myself and three other people.

In the game, you play as a robot that was sent to Mars with the purpose of making it habitable for human life. In order to help you, your creators have sent a modified species of plant along with you that can convert parts of Mars' atmosphere into oxygen. It is your job to make sure the plant has everything it needs to grow, even if that means venturing out onto the unforgiving Martian surface.
