Lauren Cunningham

Game Designer, Game Programmer, and Software Engineer

Hi, my name is Lauren and my passion is teamwork. Because I am both a programmer and an artist, I strive to get these two groups to talk to and understand one another. I believe that programmers and artists can learn a lot from each other, and that this communication can be a huge benefit to a software or videogame development team.

In addition, I am a big advocate of games that challenge a player's mind and skills (or mine while I am creating them), and I wholeheartedly enjoy forwarding this through experimentation with multi-faceted AI.
